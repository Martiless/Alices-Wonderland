About Me

Hi I am Alice. A few years ago, while listening to my sister reading I got distracted by a talking white rabbit running through our garden. I followed him and ended up falling down a rabbit hole. It felt like I was falling down that hole for days, I landed in a room with just a table and a very small door. After eating a cake that was on the table, I shrunk right down and was able to fit through the door, which lead me to Wonderland. As you can guess from the name this was an amazing place full of amazing people, creatures and plants.

After spending some time exploring wonderland I fell in love with it and wanted to share all it's wonder with you. This is why I opened Alice's Wonderland. Your one stop shop for everything Wonderland.

There are so many amazing things to see and do in Wonderland, be sure to take a look around
